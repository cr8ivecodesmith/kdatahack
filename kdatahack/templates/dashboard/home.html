About

Stop Overbudgeting! Use Market Snitch! :)

Market Snitch is a Market Research tool designed to help facilitate the creation of accurate budget estimates based on current market prices.

It's main goal is to help erradicate or minimize overbudgeting for government procurement projects.

Features

Market Price Comparison

Price Comparison against current market data

Historical Price Comparison

Price Comparison against historical price data gathered from previous awards

APIs

APIs for Organization, Awards, Bidders List, Bid Line Item and Bid Information that is periodically fetched from PhilGEPS Public Data
